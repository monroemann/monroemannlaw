T.R.U.S.T. Template

For those who have read my book T.R.U.S.T., this template will allow you to easily analyze any project you are creating to determine its 't.r.u.s.t.ability', i.e. the likelihood that someone will want to invest in it. You can also use this template to self-analyze yourself and/or any potential team member you are considering bringing on board to determine if they are really bringing something worthwhile to the project.
